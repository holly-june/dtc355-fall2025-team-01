Quiet giants live among us, carrying centuries of history in their roots and branches. Getting to the Root invites you to discover three majestic trees of the Pacific Northwest.

Each tree is a living monument whose roots carry fascinating, important stories worth telling. Whether you're a nature lover, history buff, student, or creative soul, there's a story in the roots waiting to be discovered.
