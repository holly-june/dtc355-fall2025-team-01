About

“Getting to the Root” is a collaborative digital art project that blends storytelling with interactive web design to highlight three iconic Pacific Northwest trees.

Through hand-drawn vector illustrations created by Angela Perkins and a mix of CSS animations and multimedia effects, we crafted a non-linear educational journey.

Angela's detailed illustrations form the visual heart of the site, bringing each tree's story to life. Together, we navigated the coding challenges and kept our communication clear, ensuring that this project became a true fusion of our skills.

In the end, it's an edutainment experience that invites users to explore these living landmarks in a fresh, creative way.
